The Avengers



The Avengers basially consist of 6 super heroes: Iron-man(middle), Captain America(Right), Hawkeye (Left), Thor(middle Right), Black Widow (Top), Hulk (middle left). The leader of the Avengers is Captain America, AKA Steve Rogers. His special Abilities are Super strength and combat skills. He was experimented in the 20th century, and got injected with the super-soldier serum. But when taking down his enemy, Red skull he had plane crashed in ice, only to be disconvered decades later. Then their is Iron-man. Iron man AKA Tony Stark. He used to be a weapons company CEO until he had found out what his weapons were doing he started making Iron man suits to save people rather than use bonbs. He made his first suit in a Cave out of scratch. Then after defeating his arch nemesis he made tons more suits. Then there are Hawkeye and Black Widow, AKA Clint Barton and Natasha Romanoff. They both are agents of S.H.I.E.L.D, and have various skillsets and equipment. Hawkeye is good with arrows and Black Widow is good at combat. Last but certainly not least are Thor and Hulk. Known as the 2 most powerful Avengers, Thor from Asgard is the wielder of Mjolner, his hammer. You can also actually call Thor an Alien. But Hulk on the other hand AKA Bruce Banner got hit with gamma rays and got the habit of turning into the Hulk when angry.
