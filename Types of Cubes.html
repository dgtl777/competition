Name: V-Cube 6
Created: 2004
Creator:Panagiotis Verdes

The V-Cube 6 is a 6×6×6 version of the
original Rubik's Cube. The first
mass-produced 6×6×6 was invented by
Panagiotis Verdes and is produced by the Greek company Verdes Innovations SA.

Name: 8x8
Created: Unknown
Creator:Panagiotis Verdes

The 8x8x8 cube (normally referred to
as the 8x8x8 or 8x8) is a twisty
puzzle in the shape of a cube that is cut
seven times along each of three axes.

Name: Pyramix
Introduced: 1981
Creator:Uwe Mèffert

The Pyraminx is a regular tetrahedron

made and patented by
Uwe Mèffert after the original
3 layered Rubik's Cube by Ernő Rubik

The Mirror Blocks was originally invented
