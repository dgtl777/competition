Beginner Method


Cross-Daisy

The first step for a begginer to solve the Rubiks Cube is to make a cross by making what Cubers call, the daisy. To make the daisy first find the yellow middle piece in your cube. Then put 4 white edges beside it. After that look at it in the angle which the image above is shown. Then match the edge pieces of the same color onto the middle pieces.


Then after matching the middle and edge piece, turn the front face 180deg right or left. Then keep doing the process and flip the rubiks cube to find a white cross or plus like this one.
